Automated Medical Reporting Application

The Automated Medical Reporting System is an AI-powered web application designed to assist radiologists and healthcare professionals in generating accurate and structured medical reports. The system processes diagnostic images such as X-rays, CT scans, and MRIs using computer vision techniques, while also analyzing laboratory results through intelligent data processing. Based on this analysis, the system automatically generates a draft medical report that includes clear findings, clinical interpretations, and recommendations. The generated report can then be reviewed, edited, and approved by a radiologist to ensure accuracy and patient safety. The main goal of the system is to reduce reporting time, minimize human errors, improve workflow efficiency, and support doctors in delivering faster and more reliable healthcare services.
